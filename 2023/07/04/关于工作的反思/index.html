关于工作的反思

每个人对工作的理解因人而异。对于一些人来说，工作是维持生计的手段，离开工作意味着失去收入，可能难以在社会中生存。然而，对于一些人来说情况并非如此。例如，对于那些来自富裕家庭、拥有充足财富的人来说，工作并非必须。

对于这些人来说，从财务角度来看，工作只是获取收入的一种方式。他们可以选择不工作，那么他们为什么还要去工作呢？

以我自己为例，我有以下几个原因：

  1. 工作是融入社会的一种方式。无论我们是否承认，人毕竟是社会动物，我们遵循社会标准和规范。工作是与社会直接接触的最快捷方式。通过工作，我们可以结识各种背景的人，了解不同职业的方方面面。现代工作往往需要合作与协作，与不同背景的同事相处是一种机遇和挑战。

  2. 工作是实现个人抱负的途径。人的一生并不仅仅是为了满足基本需求，我们也有精神层面的追求。对我而言，我渴望为社会创造更多的价值，希望运用自己的能力解决社会面临的问题。挑战越大的问题，越能激发我的激情。如果我解决的问题能够使更多人受益，那我就实现了个人的价值。

  3. 工作能够让人忙碌起来，暂时忘却一些不愿面对的事情。生活中会有很多困难，可能涉及亲密关系、家庭关系或个人思考引发的自我怀疑。当我们踏入办公室时，工作的引力场暂时将这些事情抛诸脑后，我们将注意力集中在工作上，这也是减少内心冲突的一种方式。

那么，什么情况下一个人会对工作感到厌倦，或者想要辞职呢？

  1. 工作环境让人身心不愉快，身体发出警告。可能是糟糕的同事关系，奇特的同事，或者你无法接受或容忍不同背景和能力的同事。与他们共事让你感到心理上的困扰。

  2. 这份工作可能无法实现个人抱负。也许你从事的是事务性工作，缺乏创新性。或者这份工作本质上并未为社会带来价值，只是为老板创造价值。你无法证明这份工作的意义所在。

  3. 工作导致更多的情绪障碍和内耗，并且身体也在发出警告。

我反思过，一个人离开学校进入社会后，打工确实是快速与社会接轨成长的一种方式。但如果没有生存的担忧，我们应更关注自我成长，不断反思眼前的工作是否能够给予足够的锻炼和价值。如果没有价值，我们应该考虑下一步，因为虚度光阴的罪过远大于多赚那点微不足道的财富。

Everyone has a different understanding of work. For some people, work is a means of livelihood, and without it, they would lose their income and struggle to survive in society. However, for others, the situation is different. If you come from a privileged background, have substantial wealth, such as being a property owner, a native resident, or if your family’s financial resources allow you to live off rental properties, stock dividends, or investment returns, then work is not a necessity.

So, for the latter group, from a financial perspective, work is merely a means to generate income. They can choose not to work, so why would they still choose to work?

Let me use myself as an example, and provide a few reasons:

Work is a way to integrate into society. Regardless of whether we admit it or not, humans are social creatures, and we adhere to certain social norms. Work is the quickest way to directly engage with society. Through work, you can meet people from various backgrounds and explore different professions. Most modern work requires collaboration and cooperation, presenting opportunities and challenges in interacting with colleagues from diverse backgrounds.

Work is a pathway to fulfill personal aspirations. Life is not solely about fulfilling basic needs; there is also a spiritual dimension. For me, I aspire to create more value for society and utilize my abilities to solve problems faced by society. The more challenging the problems, the more excited I become. If the problems I solve can benefit a larger number of people, then I have achieved my own sense of purpose.

Work keeps one occupied and helps temporarily forget things one may not want to confront. Life presents many difficulties, such as intimate relationships, family issues, or self-reflection leading to self-doubt. When you step into the workplace, the gravitational force of work temporarily pushes these concerns aside. You can focus your attention on work, often reducing internal conflicts.

Under what circumstances would someone grow tired of their work or consider resigning?

The work environment creates mental and physical discomfort, and the body sends warning signals. It could be due to poor relationships with colleagues, peculiar coworkers, or an inability to accept or tolerate colleagues from different backgrounds and abilities. Working with them causes significant psychological barriers.

The job may not align with personal aspirations. Perhaps you are engaged in operational work without much innovation. Alternatively, the job may not inherently bring value to society but only benefits the employer. You struggle to prove the significance of the work.

Work generates more emotional obstacles and internal conflicts, and the body signals warnings.

Upon reflection, when a person enters society after leaving school, working is indeed a quick way to integrate and grow. However, if survival concerns are absent, we should focus more on personal growth and constantly evaluate whether our current work provides sufficient development and value. If there is no meaningful value, it is important to consider the next steps, as wasting time is a greater loss than earning a meager amount of wealth.
